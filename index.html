About Me

I am a graduate of Linguistics & African Languages from Obafemi Awolowo University. My research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP), Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), and Large Language Models (LLMs). I focus on building AI systems for low-resource African languages, with applications in healthcare and education.

Research

My work focuses on:

  • Automatic Speech Recognition for tonal languages (Yoruba ASR thesis)
  • Proverb classification and semantic analysis in African languages
  • Sentiment analysis for mental health prediction
  • Multilingual chatbots and machine translation

Publications

  • ÒWE-YOR: Leveraging Transformer-Based Models for Yoruba Proverb Classification. Poster presented at Deep Learning Indaba 2025, Kigali, Rwanda.
  • Tone in Yoruba Automatic Speech Recognition. Undergraduate Thesis, OAU, 2025.
